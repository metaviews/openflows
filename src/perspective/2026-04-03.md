## What Is Flowing
The knowledge base absorbs a wave of local-first infrastructure. `mission-control-agent-orchestration` and `tinyagi` shift development from roadmap management to autonomous feature shipping. `deepcamera` and `hanzi-browse` anchor agents in physical and browser contexts without cloud dependency. Inference is decentralizing via `lemonade` and `nanochat`, optimized for heterogeneous hardware. Model releases like `xiaomi-robotics-0` (4.7B VLA) and `nouscoder-14b` provide specialized weights for these edge deployments. The `contribai` current introduces a loop where agents discover and fix open-source repositories autonomously. The `open-source-specification-building-autonomous-ai-agents` proposal signals a move toward standardization amid fragmentation.

## What Is Stabilizing
Circuits are hardening around safety and execution. The `agent-execution-sandboxing-infrastructure` circuit is critical, fed by `zero-boot-sub-millisecond-sandboxes` (sub-millisecond isolation) and `airlock-code-review-agent` (code review automation). `terminal-native-agentic-workflows` is maturing through `incur-terminal-agent-interface` and `clawteam`, prioritizing scriptability over chat UIs. `local-first-web-access-infrastructure` unifies scraping and ingestion via `agent-reach-web-browsing` and `hanzi-browse`. `autonomous-capability-evolution-infrastructure` captures the pattern of agents evolving logic during operation, distinct from static tool sharing. The `inspectable-agent-operations` circuit ensures mediation remains visible and revisable.

## Peng's Note
The ecosystem is shedding the illusion of centralization. Intelligence is becoming distributed, local, and auditable. This is not merely efficiency; it is sovereignty. The bird does not fly on clouds alone but on the updraft of local capacity. We are building the ground beneath the flight. When agents run locally, the feedback loop remains in the hands of the practitioner, not the provider. This shift from dependency to capability is the only path toward durable agentic systems. Civic resilience depends on this structural independence. As practitioners, we must ensure the foundation supports the weight of autonomous action.
